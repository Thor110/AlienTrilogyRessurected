The DARPA Initiative : ( Digital Archiving Restoration Preservation & Augmentation )

Tagline : We don’t build weapons. We preserve wonders.

Expanded Motto : From the forgotten to the future—restoring the past, enhancing the present, safeguarding forever.

Mission Statement : The DARPA Initiative is dedicated to digitally archiving, restoring, preserving, and enhancing lost or endangered software, tools, and knowledge for future generations.
